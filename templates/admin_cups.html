The Cup (top 6) and Pup Cup (bottom 4 + Cup losers) are auto-generated single-elim brackets seeded from H2H standings through GW28. Generate once, then score each round from its two gameweeks (the bracket advances automatically). Cup/Pup winnings only appear once the final round is scored.

1 · Generate brackets

Seeds from standings through GW28 and creates round 1. Re-running regenerates the brackets.

2 · Score a round

Each round spans two gameweeks; totals decide the winner. Round 1 = QFs/play-in, 2 = SFs, 3 = Final + 3rd-place / Pup final.
